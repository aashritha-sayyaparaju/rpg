UNDERLOST: a dungeon crawl
----------------------------

A text-based dungeon crawler built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
In this game, you wake up at the bottom of a dungeon with no memory of how you got there. Five floors stand between you and the surface, each with it's own enemies, paths, and decisions. Some of those decisions carry consequences you won't see until much later. The final floor has a dragon, and how you deal with it depends on what you did along the way.

Guide:
Each room presents a set of choices. Some lead to combat, while other lead to loot. Combat is similar to pokemon, as you and the enemy trade hits each round. You can attack, use a health potion, or try to flee (which isn't guaranteed).

Your stats update in real time at the top of the screen. The HP bar changes colors as you take damage. Gold carries over between floors and can be spent at the merchant on floor 2!

Choices beyond the immediate outcome still  matter. (Hint: read the note on floor 1!). 

Enemies:

Giant Rat
HP: 18, ATK: 4, First floor, guards something special...
Goblin Scout
HP: 30, ATK: 7, Chance to drop a dagger
Skeleton Warrior
HP: 45, ATK: 11, Patrols floor 2
Cave Troll
HP: 80, ATK: 16, Can be sneaked past on floor 3
Dungeon Witch
HP: 60, ATK: 14, Fight her or pay her
Underlost Dragon:
HP: 200, ATK: 28, Final boss on floor 5

Items:
Health Potions restore 40HP and can be used during combat. The Iron Sword, Reinforced Armor, and Lucky Charm are available from the merchant on floor 2. Lucky Charm gives a 20% change to deal double damage on any attack.

Enemeis can also drop items on death (Rusty Dagger, Bone shield, Troll Heart, Spell Shard).
